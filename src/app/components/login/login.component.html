MovieRating
MovieRating is a gigantic compendium of movies, TV shows, and video games. Its primary use is to find detailed information about any actor, producer, or piece of media content. When you pull up a movie, you'll see a synopsis, trailers, photos, a cast list, trivia, and much more. What makes MovieRating so useful is its cross-referencing. Upon opening the page for an actor, you'll see their best-known roles. Thus, MovieRating is great for those "what else have I seen her in?" moments.
